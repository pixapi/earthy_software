Magic pomodoros

by Amara Lovato

Posted on January 19, 2017 at 10:00 PM

One of the biggest discoveries during mod1 was pomodoro breaks. At the beggining I thought it was kind of weird to stop so often and I was skeptical about the benefits of it. However, after a few weeks it felt so good and refreshing to take a small pause and I noticed that when you do pomodoros you don't get burned out but makes you more productive and gives you more energy to keep working. The pomodoro technique is very simple.

  1. Set the timer and work for 25 minutes and work for that time.
  2. Take a short break (5 minutes).
  3. Work for another 25 minutes.
  4. Take another short break.
  5. Work again for 25 minutes.
  6. Take a longer break (15-20min).
If you haven't finish your tasks, restart with step 1. It's important that you make a mark one a paper to count your pomodoro 25min working sessions because after a while it's hard to keep track of how many consecutive ones you did. Good luck and have a productive day!
